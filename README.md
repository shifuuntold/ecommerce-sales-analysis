# E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

![Dashboard](images/dashboard.png)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes e-commerce sales transactions from 2022 to 2024 using Microsoft Excel. The goal was to identify revenue trends, profitability drivers, category performance, and regional sales patterns through interactive dashboard reporting.

The dashboard was built using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI cards, formulas, and slicers to provide dynamic business insights.

## Key Findings

### Category Performance

| Category | Sales | Profit |
|----------|------:|-------:|
| Electronics | 5.33M | 923K |
| Accessories | 4.25M | 736K |
| Office | 1.09M | 185K |

**Insight:** Electronics generated the highest revenue and profit, contributing approximately 50% of total sales.

---

### Regional Performance

| Region | Sales |
|--------|------:|
| West | 2.84M |
| East | 2.68M |
| South | 2.66M |
| North | 2.49M |

**Insight:** The West region was the strongest revenue contributor, while the North region recorded the lowest sales.

---

### Year-over-Year Sales Trend

| Year | Sales |
|------|------:|
| 2022 | 3.26M |
| 2023 | 3.79M |
| 2024 | 3.63M |

**Insight:** Sales increased by 16.3% from 2022 to 2023 before declining slightly by 4.3% in 2024.

---

### Quarterly Sales Trend

| Quarter | Sales |
|---------|------:|
| Q1 | 2.61M |
| Q2 | 2.70M |
| Q3 | 2.66M |
| Q4 | 2.71M |

**Insight:** Revenue remained relatively stable throughout the year, with Q4 generating the highest sales.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase investment in Electronics products due to their strong revenue and profit contribution.
2. Investigate growth opportunities in the North region.
3. Monitor the slight decline in 2024 sales performance.
4. Leverage Q4 sales trends when planning promotions and inventory allocation.
